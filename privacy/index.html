At Hyquantix Technologies Pvt. Ltd. ("Hyquantix", "we", "our", "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information, in line with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India), the Information Technology Act, 2000 and SPDI Rules, and, where applicable, the EU/EEA General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and California CCPA/CPRA.

1. Information We Collect

  • Identifiers: name, email, phone, company, job title, location.
  • Communications: messages you send via forms, email, or chat.
  • Usage data: pages viewed, device type, browser, IP address (may be truncated), timestamps.
  • Cookies & similar tech: preferences, session, analytics, marketing (subject to consent where required).
  • Recruitment data (if you apply): resume/CV, experience, portfolio links.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide and improve our websites, products, and services.
  • Respond to inquiries, support tickets, and demo requests.
  • Send updates or marketing communications (with opt-in/opt-out controls).
  • Secure our services, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.
  • Recruitment and HR processing for job applicants.

3. Lawful Basis (GDPR where applicable)

  • Consent (e.g., marketing emails, non-essential cookies).
  • Contract (to perform a contract or take steps at your request).
  • Legitimate interests (to improve services, prevent fraud, secure systems).
  • Legal obligation (to meet regulatory requirements).

4. Data Protection & Security

We use technical and organizational measures including access controls, encryption in transit, and least-privilege practices. Access to personal data is limited to authorized personnel and service providers under contract.
